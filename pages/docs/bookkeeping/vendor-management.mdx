Vendor Accounts
---------------

Vendors supply products to store and ship.

### 1\. Navigate to Vendor -> Vendor Accounts.

### 2\. Search and view all Vendor Accounts. Search conditions include:

*   Account ID.
*   Account Name.
*   Print Name.
*   Bill To.
*   Facility.

### 3\. Select an Account.
