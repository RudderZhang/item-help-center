## Sales Invoice

### 1. Navigate to Billing -> Sales Invoice.

### 2. Search and view all Invoices. Search conditions include:
- Account ID.
- Account Name.
- Print Name.
- Bill To.
- Facility.

### 3. Select an Invoice

### 4. Click Action to void, merge, or approve an invoice.

![Sales Invoice](https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/656a2fae21236d39b5805a4d/65ce966676e1919a06780dd9_salesInvoice.png)

## Add New Invoice

### 1. To add a new invoice click + Batch Post.

### 2. Enter the invoice information.

### 3. Click Add.
