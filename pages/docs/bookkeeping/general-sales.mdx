General Sales Documents
-----------------------

### 1\. Navigate to Invoice -> General Sales Documents.

![](https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/656a2fae21236d39b5805a4d/65e67609c0e30b2b8ddb6089_salesdocument.png)

### 2\. Search and view all Sales Documents. Search conditions include:

*   Customer ID.
*   Customer Name.
*   Facility.

### 3\. Select a Sales Document.

![](https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/656a2fae21236d39b5805a4d/65e765ed9d3683cc4a562971_salesdocument2.png)

### 4\. Click on the 3 dots for more actions.

*   Edit - Change details of the document.
*   Approve - Approve document for release.
*   Delete - Delete Document.
*   Download Invoice - Download the Sales Document.
*   Clone - Copy the Document.
*   Operation Log - View log for the Document.
