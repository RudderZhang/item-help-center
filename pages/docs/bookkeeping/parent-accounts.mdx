Parent Accounts
---------------

### 1\. Navigate to Billing -> Parent Accounts.

### 2\. Search and view all Accounts. Search conditions include:

*   Account Code.
*   Account Name.

![](https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/656a2fae21236d39b5805a4d/65e770bff8928252febbbb09_parentaccount.png)

Add New Parent Account
----------------------

### 1\. Click + New Parent Account.

### 2\. Enter Parent Account Information.

### 3\. Link Accounts to Parent Account.

### 4\. Click Add.
